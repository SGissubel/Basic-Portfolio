About Me

"What a long, strange trip it's been."



There are not many words that can often summarize a life more than these. At least, that is, for me. I have been so many things and traveled as well as lived so many places; but I maintain a New Jersey native.


Currently, I work as a high school Enlgish teacher--but I cannot, in good conscious, say that this defines me completely.

I am a photographer, a mountain climber, an every day adventurer, weekend worrior, motorcycle rider, gym enthusiast, life-living, joy-spreading, seeker of happiness, and appreciator of music, art, and everything that brings beauty into this world

Most importantly, at the moment I am a developer-in-training. Nights, weekends, after work, before work, and sometimes even during I relentless practice and try out my newest venture and goal--to obtain a position as a developer in a company that seeks to build and create amazing things in this fast-paced, expanding industry.
